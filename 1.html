    "What Are Plastic Credits?" Plastic credits are a certified system that quantifies the collection and recycling of plastic waste. Each credit symbolizes one ton of plastic removed from the environment, providing companies with an actionable way to meet sustainability targets. By investing in plastic credits, businesses take real steps to offset their environmental footprint and contribute to the global fight against plastic pollution.

    The Importance of Plastic Credits" Plastic credits not only drive environmental improvements but also spark social and economic progress. These credits fund essential recycling projects, create dignified jobs in waste management, and encourage innovation in reducing plastic dependency. Investing in plastic credits means being part of a global solution to reduce waste and foster sustainability.
